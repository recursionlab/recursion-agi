**The Technical Invulnerability:**

- **Arithmetization**: Based on the most elementary properties of natural numbers.
- **Constructive proof**: We can explicitly build the undecidable sentences.
- **Semantic/syntactic distinction**: Fundamental to all logic since Tarski.

**The Metamathematical Foundation:**
