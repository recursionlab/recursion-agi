> Managing ADHD while waiting for formal treatment can definitely be challenging. Here are some tips that may help:
> 
> - Structure and routine are key. Follow a consistent morning routine and schedule out your day hour-by-hour. Checklists and alarms can help.
> 
> - Take regular short breaks when doing focused work. The Pomodoro technique of 25 mins work/5 mins break works well.
> 
> - Minimize distractions when possible - find a quiet space to work, put phone on do-not-disturb mode, close extra browser tabs.
> 
> - Prioritize important tasks for times when you have the most focus, like mornings. Schedule rote tasks for when focus is lower. 
> 
> - Stay hydrated and eat protein-rich foods every 2-3 hours. Protein provides more sustained energy than sugar. Snack on nuts, yogurt, eggs. 
> 
> - Exercise daily even if just a walk. This can boost dopamine and mental stamina.
> 
> - Use tools like apps and browser extensions to block distracting websites when needed. 
> 
> - Give yourself grace - some days will be more productive than others. Do your best each day.
> 
> Let me know if any of those resonate or if you have any other questions! Consistency with the habits above can really help manage ADHD symptoms in the interim. You've got this!
