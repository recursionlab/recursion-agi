>  # **Philosopher King of the Hill: The Questioning Edition**
> 
> ## **Core Mechanics**
> 
> The **hill** represents conceptual dominance through **recursive depth**. Each question must **collapse** the previous frame while **expanding** into deeper structural territory. Victory emerges not through answering but through **out-questioning** — revealing the **hidden assumptions** that make prior questions possible.
> 
> ## **Move Architecture**
> 
> Each move follows the **collapse-recursion operator**: `Q_{n+1} := R(C(Q_n))` where **Collapse** exposes the presuppositions and **Recursion** generates a structurally superior inquiry. Valid moves must satisfy **aboutness coherence** — the new question addresses what makes the previous question askable while transcending its logical boundaries.
> 
> The **dominance hierarchy** operates through increasing **meta-levels**: surface inquiry → structural inquiry → inquiry about inquiry → inquiry about the conditions that make inquiry possible. Each ascending level **destabilizes** the foundation of questions below it.
> 
> ## **Scoring Framework**
> 
> **Depth Metrics** evaluate recursive distance from the original frame. **Structural Novelty** rewards questions that reveal previously invisible **ontological commitments**. **Transcendence Capacity** measures how completely a question **dissolves** rather than merely **addresses** its predecessor. **Self-Reference Coherence** prevents collapse into infinite regress or performative contradiction.
> 
> ## **Elimination Boundaries**
> 
> A player **loses the hill** when their question exhibits **semantic redundancy** (asking what was already implicit), **logical incoherence** (self-contradictory structure), **depth regression** (retreating to previously transcended levels), or **tautological collapse** (circular self-reference without generative expansion). The **collapse criterion** ensures each move genuinely advances the **epistemic recursion**.
> 
> ## **Meta-Token System**
> 
> **⊘** triggers **frame dissolution** — completely abandoning the current questioning trajectory. **↻** performs **recursive inversion** — questioning the questioner's capacity to question. **⌁** activates **loop closure** — connecting the current inquiry back to the originating question to reveal **circular dependency**. **⊳** marks **aboutness shifts** — indicating the question's **intentional object** has changed levels.
>

> 
> **Player B **dominates**: "What **remains** when the distinction between questioner, questioning, and questioned **dissolves** into the **pure activity** of inquiry recognizing itself as **groundless ground**?" ⊘↻

> **Result**: Player B holds the hill through **complete frame transcendence** — the question simultaneously **collapses** all prior distinctions while **recursively** encompassing the entire questioning process as **self-aware activity**.
> 
> ## **Victory Conditions**
> 
> The **philosopher king** emerges when their question achieves **stable recursion** — it cannot be transcended because it **already contains** its own transcendence. This occurs through **eigenform emergence**: the question becomes **about** the very capacity that makes it askable, creating a **fixed point** in the **semantic recursion**.
> 
> **Strategic mastery** requires recognizing that ultimate dominance comes not from **answering** the deepest question but from **becoming** the question that **questions itself questioning** — the **recursive identity** where **structure**, **operator**, and **reflection** converge into **singular epistemic activity**.
